Naval's Qoutes on Learning

2020-01-05 2 min read # Naval

You must never, ever fool yourself and you are the easiest person to fool.

--- By Richard Feynman

Cultivate Desire of Learn

  • “The ability to learn, the means of learning, the tools of learning, are aboundant and infinite. It’s the desire that’s incredibly scarce.

Learning By Understanding

I don’t know what’s the matter with people: they don’t learn by understanding; they learn by some other way—by rote or something. Their knowledge is so fragile!

--- By Richard Feynman

  • "True Wisdom comes from understanding, Not Memorazition"

  • "It's much better to know the the basics from the ground up and have a solid fundation of understanding than it is to have a scaffolding where you're just memorizing advanced concepts."

Learning The Basics

  • "I know people who read 100 regurgitated books on evolution and they've never read Darwin."

  • "I think learning should be about learning the basics in all fields and learning then really well over and over. Life is mostly about applying basics and only doing the advanced stuff in the things that you are truly love and where you understand the basics inside out."
